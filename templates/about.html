About

This app is designed so that students don't have to manually enter in tasks into their task management systems at the beginning of every quarter/semester. Just enter in the URL for your course's syllabus page on Canvas and the app will scrape the syllabus and output a CSV that can be imported into the task management system of your choice (Todoist or Asana). The CSV contains the task name and due date for every task on the syllabus.

For guidance on how to import the CSV into Todoist or Asana, please refer to the documentation below:

  • Todoist documentation
  • Asana documentation
